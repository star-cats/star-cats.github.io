BLINKY*DOME

Our 32’ geodesic dome is visible along the Black Rock City skyline from the man.

The top of the dome is outfitted with 80 custom acrylic triangle diffusers with over 10,000 individually-addressable, programmable LEDs.

These are driven by 5 networked, custom-designed and fabricated StarPusher controllers.

The dome lighting patterns can be customized to follow pre-programmed patterns or be sound responsive with the dome's sound system.

Our 14’ tall light sculpture, in the shape of a Zome, serves as a space for those seeking to escape into a visual vortex or showcase their musical tastes.

It is made of a wood and custom-fabricated metal connector skeleton, covered with LED neon flex along its edges, both inside and out. With 10,000+ addressable LEDs, it’ll catch passersby lovingly in its trippy trap.

With your help in design and implementation, Dragon Egg can have an ever-changing kaleidoscope of symmetric yet non-uniform visual effects that feel magical.
