For existing *cats

Bringing a +1

If you want to bring someone new into camp, here's the process.

  • Write an intro email to the *cats groupExplain who your +1 is, how you know them, and anything relevant to Burning Man or camp. Include pics if you want.
  • Wait 48 hoursSee if any current *cats respond — publicly or privately — with questions or objections about your +1.
  • If no objections, add them to the Google GroupThen loop them into the email thread where you introduced them. They may get a silly question from an existing *cat as a vibe check.
  • Get them set upMake sure they see the new cat guide, fill out the roster survey (forms.gle/haLEkkH5L93ibm9H6), and join Discord (discord.gg/3sReU2z6p).
  • Teach them our ways!!
